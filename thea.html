🤍🦌 Oh Deer Thea 🦌🤍

Trekkie Cosplayer | Dreamer of Stars | Collector of Cozy Moments

Hi, I’m Thea.
I’m just a girl who loves Star Trek,
loves dressing up as my favorite characters,
and finds joy in pretending I command a starship named the USS Solenne.

Cosplay is my way of stepping into a world of exploration, connection, and adventure.
Sometimes it’s a convention hallway. Sometimes it’s just my living room.
Either way, it’s where I feel at home —
among phasers, combadges, and the hope of the final frontier.

Whether I’m in uniform or pajamas,
I’m always looking for the next story,
the next spark of joy,
the next reason to say, “Computer, set course.”
