🤍🦌 Thea — Ranger of the Wilds & the Stars 🌿🚀

National Park Ranger | Cosplayer | Forest Dreamer | Starship Adventurer

By day, Thea wears the pine‑green uniform of a National Park Ranger, tending to the wild places where deer slip through the mist and the wind carries the scent of cedar and rain. Her days are filled with guiding visitors along winding trails, watching over fragile ecosystems, and quietly protecting the quiet magic of the forest.

But in her other life — at conventions, in creative studios, and under the shimmer of starlit photo shoots — Thea steps into a whole other kind of uniform: Starfleet captain, alien princess, and countless other sci‑fi heroines she brings to life through cosplay. Each costume is a bridge between her two worlds — the grounded beauty of Earth's nature and the boundless curiosity of the final frontier.

Star Trek fuels her imagination, reminding her that exploration is as much about compassion as it is discovery. The forest reminds her that some of the greatest adventures can be found just beyond the next hill. Whether she's patrolling a ridge line, leading a nature talk, or captaining a starship through unknown space, Thea is always seeking the next horizon.
