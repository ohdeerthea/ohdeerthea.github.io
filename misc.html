Miscellaneous Characters

A Collection of Diverse Original Characters

About This Collection

This gallery features a diverse collection of original characters that span various genres, settings, and artistic explorations. From one-shot character designs to experimental concepts, this section showcases the breadth and creativity of character development beyond the main trio.

You'll find characters from different universes and timelines here - cyberpunk hackers, medieval knights, modern-day supernatural investigators, space explorers, and fantasy creatures. Each character represents a unique story possibility and artistic challenge, exploring different themes, aesthetics, and narrative potential.

Some characters featured here may graduate to having their own dedicated galleries if they develop into larger stories or commission series. This collection serves as both a showcase of artistic variety and a testing ground for new character concepts and visual styles.
